Paris, France

Frenchxperience

Why are there here more dogs than kids? Why not all that looks a baguette is a baguette? Where are Parisian taxis? Who counts the trees in the city? These are the questions to some of the most interesting curiosities of the French capital city. I do not know about you, but I love the place.

Meiringen, Switzerland

Swiss sweet tooth haven

Meiringen Meringues define sweet as a notion. But they are not the only sweet thing about this place in Jungfrau region of the Alps. Go find out why Sherlock Holmes couldn't have possibly survived the fall down the waterfall or enjoy the Via Alpina trail. Worth visiting.

Croatia, Plitvice

Nature's wonder

This UNESCO recognized natural park around and between a thousand of lakes lets you gorget you're still on Earth. Heavenly nature colors provide some unforgettable moments. Great destination all year round.

Moleson, Switzerland

Fribourg Ski Gate

2002 meters high, an icon by itself, Moleson is an Alpine peak that can be disovered by hiking, climbing or cable car. Via Ferrata highly recommended.

Vevey, Switzerland

All about food?

Charlie Chaplin or milk chocolate - who was first in Vevey? Why is there a huge fork stuck in the lake? Vevey is a pictoresque city in Swiss riviera on the shore of Lake Leman. Along a mediterranean promenade you can enjoy the views on the Alps mirrored in the blue waters of the lake.

Grindelwald, Switzerland

Year-round marvel

Why is Grindelwald called a 'glacier village'? Is it true that the total length of ski pistes is equal to the size of Switzerland from north to south? Grindelwald stole my heart the first time I looked out of the wooden window frame of a wooded mountain hut and saw the village full of light in the middle of a snow clad vallay. A treasure.

Bali, Indonesia

Another face of Indonesia

Is there a kind of rice in the world that you cannot find on Bali? Is there as many shrines on the island as there are households? Why is Bali called the island of the happy people? Bali teaches you the trust in fate as soon as you join the traffic. But you are already in paradise, aren't you?

Beziers, France

Watergate

Discover how to move a boat up by 21 meters using water and bring it back down. Beautiful town famous for a massacre of its citizens in the 13th century.

Malta

Honey honey

Be sure to stick to the right (left) side of the road while you hunt down the locations from Gladiator, Troy or Game of Thrones.

Gletterens, Switzerland

Swiss sand beach

A nice place to visit when looking for a bathing spot at Lake Neuchatel. Next to a natural bird reserve park and a small harbour, Gletterens depicts a typically Swiss low profile gem.

Lausanne, Switzerland

Olympic HQ

Try getting into one of 40 nightclubs (treat is as a challenge) or search for haute-culture venues - this city has a lot to offer. Or just go down the hill on rollerblades and beat the 80 kph record.

New York, USA

Crazy enough?

The Island of Hills or New Amsterdam? How much is 35 square meters apartment in the city? Was Pizza really invented in Italy? How many of 800 languages spoken in NYC do you speak? New York is as unique as the people who live there. They say you take photos of the locals on your first day there. On your second day, you just laugh. On the third you kind of look similar. On the fourth, people start to take pictures of you.

Benitachell, Spain

Sun and tan

Great food and sun all year long. Does it sound interesting already?

Valais, Switzerland

Alpine Crown

Heaven for the mountain lovers in all forms: hikers, climbers, MTB riders and roadies, bikers and sports car drivers.
