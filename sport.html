My sports


My deal with sports is a rough ride one. I cannot do without but I do tend to suffer once I am doing it. Such a love-hate relationship. In general, I am a multi-sport type of a person. What I am into the most is the effort. Routine is what I like the most as it rings my bell of a person who is highly addictive. I would do running, cycling, swimming a lot once preparing for a triathlone event. I would lift weights too from time to time for a period of several weeks. I can hike but I would rather trail run the same route.


My trainings


I run and ride the bike from home mostly. This saves loads of time but eliminates the variety of routs possible. I do go to the Alps to climb during summer time though. Nothing could potentially replace that for me. I can swim in a lake as soon as the water temperature allows it. For majority of time, I do my swim sessions in a swimming pool.
